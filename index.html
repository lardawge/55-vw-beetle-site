Overview

This 1955 Volkswagen Type 1 Oval Window Beetle is a rare California-delivered example retaining its matching body, pan, and front beam, verified by an official Volkswagen Museum Birth Certificate. Originally sold through the legendary Competition Motors in Hollywood, the car has remained in California its entire life before being discovered in storage in Valencia.

The Beetle has undergone extensive restoration work, including a fully restored rolling chassis, completed metalwork, and the first stage of bodywork completed on the majority of the car, ready for next steps to make it that perfect driver or shop-level restoration. Nearly all sheet metal is original, and the included inventory of components is comprehensive—making this an ideal foundation for a high-quality restoration, whether performed by a DIY builder or a professional shop.

An optional 1954 Okrasa 36hp engine built by Mr Okrasa (Joe Ruiz) is available separately for a buyer seeking a period-correct performance upgrade.
